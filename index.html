Strategy Selection

Three planning options for the same client situation. Mark each as Interested or Not Interested as you walk through them — the recommended option has a darker border and a "Recommended" badge.

STRATEGY 01
Sell Now (Year 1) Recommended
Close in the current tax year. Brooklyn is deployed at sale, generating short-term losses that absorb the long-term capital gain immediately.
STRATEGY 02
Seller Finance (Jan 1 next year) Recommended
Negotiate the sale to close on January 1 of next year. No insurance product — the buyer signs a contract that simply closes later. Brooklyn engages with a full Y1 of losses on the new tax year.
STRATEGY 03
Structured Sale Recommended
Insurance-product wrapper that defers the gain across multiple tax years. Brooklyn losses absorb each year's recognized chunk; basis cash is paid at sale and reinvested. Best fit for large gains where multi-year deferral materially improves net.
