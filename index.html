Strategy Selection

Three planning options for the same client situation. Mark each as Interested or Not Interested as you walk through them — the recommended option has a darker border and a "Recommended" badge.

STRATEGY 01
Recommended

Sell Now

Close the sale in the current tax year and deploy Brooklyn immediately to absorb the long-term capital gain.

Cash Received in full at close
Lockup None
Default Risk None — buyer pays at sale
STRATEGY 02
Recommended

Seller Finance

Negotiate a closing date later in the year (typically Jan 1 of next year). Brooklyn opens on a fresh tax year with a full year of losses to deploy against the sale.

Cash Paid by buyer at the agreed closing date
Lockup Only the months until closing
Default Risk Buyer could fall through before closing
STRATEGY 03
Recommended

Structured Sale

Insurance-product wrapper that defers the gain across multiple tax years. Brooklyn absorbs each year’s recognized chunk as it’s released. Best when multi-year deferral materially improves the net.

Cash Basis at sale, gain over years
Lockup 18 month minimum lockup
Default Risk Insurance carrier guarantees payouts
